Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our travel services and booking process.

Booking is easy! Simply search for your desired destination, select your dates and preferences, choose from our curated options, and complete the secure checkout process. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details of your booking.

Our cancellation policy varies by booking type. Most tours and activities can be cancelled up to 24 hours before the start time for a full refund. Hotels typically allow free cancellation up to 24-48 hours before check-in. Please check the specific cancellation policy for your booking during the checkout process.
